Progressive Rock/Metal November 2025

Complexity, Atmosphere, and the Art of the Long Song

Lately, I've been lost in the intricate rhythms of progressive metal. It's not just about the technicality; it's about the emotional journey these tracks take you on. From the mathematical precision of Tool to the melancholic atmosphere of Porcupine Tree, my current playlist is a testament to music that demands attention.

There's something profound about a 17-minute epic like Anesthetize or the philosophical depth of Lateralus. These aren't just songs; they are architectural marvels of sound.
